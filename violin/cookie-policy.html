What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to website owners.

How we use cookies

This website (raquelviolin.com) uses a minimal number of cookies to ensure the basic functionality of the site. We do not use cookies for advertising or tracking purposes.

Third-party cookies

This website embeds content from third-party services that may set their own cookies:

  • YouTube - We embed YouTube videos on our site. YouTube may set cookies on your device when you interact with the video player. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Spotify - We embed Spotify players on our music page. Spotify may set cookies on your device. For more information, see Spotify's Privacy Policy.
  • Formsubmit.co - We use Formsubmit.co to process contact form submissions. For more information, see Formsubmit.co.

Your rights

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the ePrivacy Directive, you have the right to:

  • Know what cookies are being used and why
  • Refuse non-essential cookies
  • Delete cookies from your browser at any time

How to manage cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Here is how to manage cookies in the most common browsers:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies
  • Firefox: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and Site Permissions

Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of this website.
